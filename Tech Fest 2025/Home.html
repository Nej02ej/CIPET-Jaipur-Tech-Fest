Here are the detailed rules & guidelines for the CAD Modeling Competition:

• Time Limit: Complete the 3D modeling task within the allocated time frame.

Draft to Craft

Date - 07.04.2025 (12:00PM - 01:00 PM)

“Draft to Craft” in plastic recycling aim to foster innovation, efficiency, & sustainability in recycling processes, products, & policies. They involve creating solutions that are environmentally responsible, economically viable, & scalable for reducing plastic waste in the long run. Plastic recycling focuses on moving from theoretical solutions to tangible, real-world applications that can reduce plastic waste, improve recycling systems, & create sustainable alternatives.


DUO or SOLO

More Info

Here are the detailed rules & guidelines for the Draft to Craft



RULES


Eligibility


• The competition is open to all students of the college.

• Participants can register as individuals or in teams (max 2 members per team).


Theme & Objective


• Participants must create a functional or decorative product using plastic waste.

• The final product should be innovative, eco-friendly, & demonstrate effective plastic waste reuse.


Allowed Materials


• Only plastic waste (Working & non- working model)

• Other supporting materials like glue, paint, thread, or wood can be used.



Process & Timelines


• Idea Submission: Teams must submit a brief proposal.

• The concept & inspiration Materials used Practical use or impact.


Judging Criteria


• Innovation & Creativity – Uniqueness & originality of the craft

• Effective Use of Plastic Waste– How much plastic waste is repurposed

• Functionality– Usability of the product

• Sustainability– Environmental impact of the project


Prohibited Activities


• No pre-made plastic items should be used except waste materials.

• Projects must be safe & non-toxic (no harmful chemicals or unsafe processes).

Gather your friends & join the unforgettable "Hunter Hunk" experience!


TRIO or SQUAD

More Info

Here are the detailed rules & guidelines for the Hunter Hunk


CHALLENGE DESCRIPTION


Form a team of 3 to 4 members & dive into the thrilling "Hunter Hunk" competition! Tackle a series of challenges, with each clue unlocking the next step toward the hidden Hunter. Missing a clue could cost victory, so stay sharp & ready for surprises. This adventure is about showcasing teamwork, problem-solving, & embracing the journey. Gather your courage & embark on an unforgettable quest for the ultimate prize!


RULES


• Each team must consist of 3 to 4 members.

• The Hunter Hunk comprises 5 clues, with each clue leading to the next in a specific sequence. Teams must follow this sequence; skipping a clue will result in disqualification.

• Clues must be sought only in designated areas; searching in off-limit areas is prohibited.

• If a team accidentally discovers another team's clue, they must not interfere with it; any tampering will lead to disqualification.

• Participants must return any objects moved during the search to their original positions.

• While all teams have different clues located in various places, the final destination (the hunter) remains the same. The team that finds the hunter first wins

• Decisions made by the judges are considered final & binding

More Info

Here are the detailed rules & guidelines for the Gear Up Challenge



RULES


• Team Formation: Participants can compete individually or in teams (as per event guidelines).

• Provided Tools: Only the tools given by the organizers can be used - no personal tools allowed.

• Bicycle Model: All teams will work on the same model of a bicycle to ensure fairness.
